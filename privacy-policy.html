Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 13, 2026

Welcome to TamilFMOnline. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the data we may collect through your use of our mobile application.

1. Information Collection and Use

Our application is designed to provide streaming radio services. We do not require you to create an account or provide personal details like your name or phone number to use the app.

However, we use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you (such as Advertising IDs):

  • Google Play Services: Required for app updates and core functionality.
  • AdMob (Google Ads): Used to show advertisements to keep the app free. AdMob uses cookies and device identifiers to show relevant ads.
  • Firebase Analytics: Used to monitor app performance and crashes so we can fix bugs quickly.

2. Data Handling & Security

We do not sell your data to third parties. Any data processed by third-party tools (like Google AdMob) is encrypted in transit using industry-standard protocols.

3. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us immediately.

4. Third-Party Streams

The radio stations provided in this app are hosted by third parties. When you play a station, you are connecting to their servers. These external sites have their own privacy policies which we do not control.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
