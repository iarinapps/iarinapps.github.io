Our Story

Iarin Apps was founded with a simple mission: to build high-quality, lightweight, and accessible mobile applications that serve regional communities. We noticed that many Tamil listeners struggled to find a reliable, clean, and modern platform to listen to their favorite FM stations—so we built Tamil Radios Online.

Since then, we have evolved into a full-scale Web and Mobile Development Company, helping businesses transform their ideas into functional digital products. Based on the latest Android API 35 standards, our apps are designed for performance, speed, and security.

Our Values

  • 🚀 Innovation: Using the latest tech stack (Java, Room, Jetpack).
  • 🎧 Experience: Focused on crystal-clear streaming and UI.
  • 🌍 Reach: Bringing Tamil culture to users across the globe.

The "Tamil Radios" Vision

Our flagship app is more than just a player; it's a companion for the Tamil diaspora. From Chennai to Singapore, and London to Toronto, we ensure that every Tamil soul stays connected to their roots through news, music, and talk shows.
